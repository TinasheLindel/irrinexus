Basin nexus gains (iGains4Gains)
After Lankford et al. (2025) — water allocation, real water savings, paracommoner redistribution
iGains4Gains logic applied to Rweru basin context. Four paracommoners: proprietor (irrigation), neighbour (downstream reuse), society, and nature. Real water savings = aggregate depletion reduction T1→T2. Rebound risk flagged when efficiency gains lead to area expansion without withdrawal controls.

Health safety index (HSI)
P3 wastewater-reuse schemes · water source × treatment level interactions
HSI = composite of pathogen reduction efficiency, treatment compliance rate, and exposure pathway control. Runs 0–1 (higher = safer). Derived from water source baseline + treatment efficacy modifier. WHO restricted irrigation guideline threshold: HSI ≥ 0.75. HSI drives the fourth axis of the WEFHI sustainability polygon for P3 schemes.
