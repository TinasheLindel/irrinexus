Basin-scale outcomes
iGains4Gains logic (Lankford et al. 2025) — real water savings, paracommoner redistribution, rebound risk
Basin outcomes are the direct hydrological consequence of the field-scale transition decision. Freed water does not automatically benefit nature — it depends on whether withdrawal rules are enforced. This section makes that linkage explicit.

Economics & water tariffs
Capital costs · O&M · NPV · payback · volumetric water tariff design for P4 metered schemes
Tariff design and economic viability are inseparable under modernised P4 irrigation. Water metering enables volumetric pricing — the primary governance instrument for preventing the rebound effect and recovering O&M costs.

Health safety index (HSI)
P3 wastewater-reuse schemes · water source × treatment × exposure pathway
HSI is the fourth axis of the WEFHI polygon for P3 schemes. It is derived from water source characteristics and treatment efficacy — not set independently. The HSI penalty is the quantitative cost of inadequate treatment.
WEF+H nexus polygons
WEFNI (3-axis P1/P2/P4) or WEFHI (4-axis P3) · Taguta et al. (2022) methodology
Polygon area integrates all nexus dimensions into a single visual index. The WEFHI extension makes the health cost of poor treatment visible as area collapse on the HSI axis.
